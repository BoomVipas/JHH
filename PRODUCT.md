# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Thai and Thai-Chinese families and shop owners who buy joss paper offerings (kongtek) wholesale and retail, especially around Cheng Meng (เช็งเม้ง), Sart Chin, and ancestor-worship rituals. They arrive from LINE/social links on phones and desktops. Their job: browse the catalogue, feel the quality and breadth of the range, and contact the shop to order. Secondary audience: the founding family themselves, evaluating this POC as the future face of a decades-old business.

## Product Purpose

A proof-of-concept landing page for จิงเฮียงฮั้ง (placeholder hanzi 金香行), a family joss-paper company. It is a cinematic single-scroll product catalogue: 26 real products re-photographed into heavenly realms, presented with scroll-driven storytelling. Success = the family sees their everyday warehouse products elevated into something worthy of the ritual they serve, and a visitor can reach any product and the contact CTA effortlessly.

## Brand Personality

Gilded, ceremonial, ascending. The offerings are burnt to carry love upward to ancestors; the page should feel like that journey — leaving the earthly shop floor and rising through clouds. Reverent but warm, festival-rich but never cluttered. Thai voice first, quiet English accents.

## Anti-references

- "Chinese restaurant menu" clutter: red-and-gold everything, dragons on every border, Ma Shan Zheng-style casual brush fonts.
- Generic AI landing slop: centered hero over mesh gradient, three equal feature cards, eyebrow labels over every section.
- E-commerce grid dullness: Shopee/Lazada-style dense product tiles with prices and badges. This is a brand story, not a checkout.

## Design Principles

1. **The product is the hero.** Every scene exists to glorify one real product; motion and background serve it, never compete with it.
2. **One continuous ascent.** The page is a single journey upward through realms; every transition hands off to the next (no hard section resets).
3. **Gold is a material, not a color.** Render it with gradients, sheen and light; flat yellow is forbidden.
4. **Motion is the narrative.** Scroll = playhead. If an animation doesn't advance the ascent story or answer a user action, cut it.
5. **Reverence over spectacle.** These objects are for honouring ancestors; the tone stays dignified even at maximum cinematic effect.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- `prefers-reduced-motion`: full static fallback (no pinning, no scrub, no autoplay) with all content readable in document order.
- Thai text always real HTML text (never baked into images), WCAG AA contrast on all copy, larger-than-default body size for older buyers.
- Interactive wheel is progressive enhancement; every product remains reachable by plain scrolling.
